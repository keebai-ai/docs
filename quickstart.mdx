## Create your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up">
    Go to [keebai.com](https://keebai.com) and click **Get Started**. You can sign up with your email or Google account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your plan">
    Select the plan that fits your needs. You can start with the **Free** tier to explore the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your workspace">
    Give your workspace a name and invite team members if you want to collaborate.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create your first project

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the dashboard">
    Once logged in, you'll see your dashboard. Click the **New Project** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a template">
    Choose from our library of templates or start from scratch. Templates are pre-configured for common use cases like content creation, data analysis, and customer support.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your assistant">
    Give your project a name and describe what you want the AI to help you with. The more context you provide, the better the results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start using it">
    That's it! Your AI assistant is ready. Type your first prompt and see the magic happen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can always edit your project settings later. Don't worry about getting everything perfect on the first try.
</Tip>
